WELCOME TO PHILEDGER

PhiLedger is a state-of-the-art synthwave productivity environment designed to bridge the gap between deep work and gamified progression. This is not just a dashboard; it is your digital cockpit for conquering the algorithm.

CORE SYSTEMS

  • HYPERDRIVE TIMER (Pomodoro) Engage the Hyperdrive to enter a distraction-free 'Flow State'. When active, the UI strips away distractions and accelerates the retro-grid. Complete sessions to earn massive XP.
  • MISSION LOG & BOSS FIGHTS Your tasks are your missions. Standard missions grant base XP, but marking a task as a "BOSS" initiates a high-stakes encounter requiring multiple completions to 'defeat', rewarding you with elite XP gains.
  • MISSION TIMELINE A linear time-blocking interface. Drag and drop your missions onto the timeline to schedule your day. The scanner line tracks your real-time progress across the 24-hour cycle.
  • GAMIFIED RANKING Scale the ranks from Iron to Radiant. Your progress is physically represented by your horizon vehicle's position. Reach the finish line to advance to the next tier.
  • PHILEDGER-OS AI A context-aware intelligent assistant. The AI monitors your current missions, timeline, and rank in real-time, providing personalized strategic advice and focus tips tailored to your specific workload.

SYSTEM MAINTENANCE: All data is stored locally in your browser's neural net (LocalStorage). No external surveillance active.
