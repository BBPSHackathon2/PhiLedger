PhiLedger Pilot’s Manual:

1. Task Initialization
Action: Go to the Mission Log panel.
Feature: Enter your tasks into the input field.
Pro Tip: Toggle the Boss Mode checkbox for high-stakes tasks. Bosses have HP (Health Points) and require multiple clicks to defeat, rewarding you with significantly more XP.
2. The Timeline
Action: Drag and Drop your missions from the log onto the Mission Timeline.
Feature: This allows you to visualize your path for the day. You can click slotted missions to remove them if plans change. 
3. Focus Mode
Action: Adjust the timer using the + / - buttons and hit Start Engine.
Feature: Engaging the timer triggers Flow State.
The UI declutters (hiding secondary panels) to eliminate distractions.
A Spotify Launcher appears, allowing you to quickly bridge into your focus playlist.
4. Assistance
Action: Click the pulsing Icon in the bottom-left corner.
Feature: Interact with PhiLedger. You can ask the AI for a status report, motivation, or productivity tips. It is aware of your current Rank, XP, and active missions.
5. Gamification
Action: Complete missions and finish Pomodoro sessions.
Feature: Every action earns XP. Watch the Horizon Car move closer to the finish line as you gain experience.
As you hit XP milestones, your Rank Badge will evolve (Iron → Bronze → ... → Radiant).
The Finish Line dynamically updates to show the name of your next rank.
6. The Archives
Action: Click the System Archive (Cassette icon) button.
Feature: This opens the Cassette Archive. Each day’s progress is saved as a digital cassette. You can click them to "playback" (view) the stats from that specific date.
7. Weekly Review
Action: Scroll to the bottom of the main dashboard.
Feature: The Weekly Stat Panel aggregates your performance over the last 7 days.

Quick Start Summary:
Add a task.
Drag it to a time slot.
Start the timer.
Defeat the task to earn XP.
Rank up and repeat.
